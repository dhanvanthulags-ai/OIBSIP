About me

I'm a 2nd-year B.E. CSE (AI & ML) student at Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology, Pollachi. I like building things that turn tedious manual work into something automated — whether that's organizing a messy folder of documents or turning a 2-hour lecture into 5 minutes of notes.

I've built projects across NLP, speech-to-text, and applied generative AI, and competed in hackathons including the GenAI Hackathon at KPR Institute and the TCE Ideathon at Thiagarajar College of Engineering. I'm always looking for the next interesting problem to automate.
